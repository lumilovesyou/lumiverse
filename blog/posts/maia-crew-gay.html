While not the only inspiration for this go around of the site it's certainly the biggest. I really liked the simple design and layout of the page. I wanted my own retro-style site but kept getting further from it I felt. Seeing Maia's page inspired me to try and keep the design of my own simple. I felt that actually helped more in making it feel retro than trying to style it off of the wacky over-designed pages that were popular.

I don't have too much more to say other than I really liked her use of superscript and decided to do the same. If you haven't seen her page I'll link it here or you can click on her 88x31 below!
